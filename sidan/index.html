ARE YOU EXPERIENCED?

Released May 1967

Musicians:

  • Jimi Hendrix, Guitar & Vocals
  • Mitch Mitchell, Drums & Backing Vocals
  • Noel Redding, Bass guitar & Backing Vocals

Are you experienced is the debut studio album by the Jimi Hendrix Experience. Released in 1967, the LP was an immediate critical and commerical success, and it is widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of all time. The album features Jimi Hendrix's innovative approach to songwriting and electric guitar playing which soon established a new direction in psychedelic and hard rock music.

    Side One

    1. Foxy Lady

    2. Mani Depression

    3. Hey Joe

    4. Love or Confusion

    5. May This Be Love

    6. I Don´t Live Today

    Side Two

    1. The Wind Cries Mary

    2. Fire

    3. 3rd Stone from the Sun

    4. Foxey Lady

    5. Are You Expirenced?

In 2013, Mojo magazine listed "Are You Exprienced" as the greatest guitar album of the time.

The budget for the recordings was rather limited so Chas Chandler (producer) and Hendrix completed much of the album´s pre-production work at their shared apartment.

Chas Chandler,
Producer

Are You Experienced sold more than one million copies whithin seven months of its release.
